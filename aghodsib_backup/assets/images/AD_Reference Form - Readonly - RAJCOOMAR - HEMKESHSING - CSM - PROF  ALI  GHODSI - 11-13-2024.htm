To the Admissions Committee,

I am pleased to recommend Yush Rajcoomar, who was a student in my class during the Fall 2021 term. Yush's performance stood out among his peers, achieving a final grade of 92 and placing him 4th out of 50 students. This accomplishment was particularly notable given the challenges of the online learning environment.

For his course project, Yush collaborated with another student to classify cancer patches using a histopathology dataset. This project showcased his ability to work with real-world data and implement machine learning models effectively. A review of the associated GitHub repository highlights his meaningful contributions to the codebase. Yush demonstrated proficiency in Python, scikit-learn, and machine learning workflows, playing an active role in developing the project’s classification pipeline.

In class, Yush exhibited technical competence and strong teamwork skills—both essential in the field of data science. His ability to bridge theory with practical application is a testament to his well-rounded capabilities. Currently, Yush works as a Machine Learning Engineer, focusing on spatial interpolation in mining. This role demands advanced statistical techniques and reflects his ability to apply machine learning across domains, from healthcare to mining.

Given his academic excellence, technical expertise, and industry experience, I am confident that Yush Rajcoomar will excel in the Master of Science in Applied Computing program at the University of Toronto. His background makes him well-suited to succeed in an advanced computing environment, where practical application of machine learning plays a critical role.
